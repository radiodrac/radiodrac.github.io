Hello, my name is Urszula

I'm a beginner web developer aspiring to create beautiful and intuitive to use websites and apps.

My interest in web development started in middle school when personal blogs were popular among teenagers. I didn't have internet access at the time, so not knowing how CMS platforms work, I thought everyone builds their pages from scratch. And so, the 13-year-old me installed a shareware HTML editor (with a built-in tutorial) and started learning, dreaming of becoming a fulfilled developer or programmer one day.

Although my ongoing career took a slightly different path, this childhood dream of mine has never died. I'm currently learning best practices with freeCodeCamp while expanding my knowledge by attending conferences and workshops. I'm confident in HTML and CSS, know basic JavaScript, PHP, SQL, and Java, and am motivated to learn new technologies and languages.

In addition to coding, I also love drawing and playing games on my laptop or smartphone in my spare time.

Feel free to reach out to me!
